About Us

Hey everyone! We're AJ and Nick, and we run a joint set we like to call “KAIZENOVA”. We started playing at our fraternity parties and now have been playing in clubs and events around the East Coast. We play a mix of trendy songs, throwbacks and EDM to bring that high energy for the young adult crowd, and will ensure a memorable night for everyone! Expect an electrifying atmosphere that ensures the party goes on all night, and can't wait to play for you guys soon!
